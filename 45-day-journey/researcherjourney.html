Introduction

In early February 2025, I discovered an untapped part of my identity: research. In the stock market, I was a true analyst, leveraging fundamental, technical, and news analysis to evaluate stocks. This passion carried over when I transitioned fully to the crypto market. In crypto, I combined technical, fundamental, and narrative analysis, but I felt I hadn’t yet reached my full potential as an analyst. Thus, in February, I decided to sharpen and expand my crypto analysis skills. I began by learning on-chain analysis and building a foundational understanding of Web3 from the ground up. I aim to apply my research to drive DeFi innovation and decentralized ecosystem growth. Let’s embark on this journey.
